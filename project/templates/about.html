About this project

During my time working in the medical field, I noticed that a great deal of patients experienced anxiety after being newly diagnosed.

While providers often guide patients through their diagnosis, many still feel overwhelmed with the implications of their new situation.

I created this website to facilitate communication between patients who experience the similar symptoms.

The porpose of this is two-fold:
1) To help people learn and share tips on how to cope with symptoms.
2) To alliviate the feelings of loneliness and anxiety that patients experience.

This website may also have applications for research as it could allow researchers to query information about treatment outcomes in specific locations and times.
